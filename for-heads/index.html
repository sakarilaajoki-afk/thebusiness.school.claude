What the product is not

A few honest clarifications to save you a qualifying email:

  • It is not a homework platform. Students play together in the same classroom, not independently from home.
  • It is not an individual-study tool. Every decision is made in a live session, not in solo practice (solo mode is available for revision only).
  • It is not a Kahoot-style quiz. The quiz is one component; the main activity is decision-making under time pressure.
  • It does not replace teaching. It complements it with a lived experience of pricing, finance and competition.
