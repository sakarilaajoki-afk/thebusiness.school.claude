What you are signing off

Three questions a Head asks — answered

Does it raise attainment?

It rehearses the exact skills the papers reward — analysis, evaluation and judgement under time pressure — because every decision is scored against the Edexcel specification. Students argue trade-offs, not recall definitions.

Can I evidence engagement?

Every session produces student and class reports automatically — a record of participation and progress you can bring to observations, learning walks and department reviews without extra marking.

Does it fit the budget?

One licence covers the department — per school, or per MAT, for the year. Never per seat, never per student. You run a free classroom pilot first, so the decision is made on evidence, not a brochure.
