Designed to Vistaprint UK specification (85 × 55 mm with 2 mm bleed). Ready for the Schools & Academies Show, London — 7 May 2026.

How to generate the print files (2 minutes)

  1. Click Print / Save as PDF (top right) or press Ctrl+P (Windows) / ⌘+P (Mac).
  2. In the print dialog, set Destination to "Save as PDF".
  3. Set Paper size to Custom → 89mm × 59mm. (Most browsers let you pick this; if not, choose A4 and adjust in the next step.)
  4. Set Margins to None and Scale to 100%.
  5. Enable Background graphics (crucial — otherwise the navy background won't print).
  6. Save the PDF. You'll get two pages: page 1 = front, page 2 = back.
  7. On Vistaprint: upload the PDF, choose "Standard UK business card — 85 × 55 mm", and select the front + back print option.

Schools & Academies Show checklist — 7 May 2026

  1. Order on Vistaprint by 28 April for standard UK delivery in time for the show.
  2. Suggested paper: Matte 400 gsm or Premium Matte Laminated — premium feel without gloss glare under exhibition lighting.
  3. Order 200 pcs minimum — exhibition traffic moves quickly.
  4. Test the QR code with your phone after printing (hold ~15 cm away, ambient light). Re-print if scan fails.
  5. Bring a second stack in a different jacket pocket — cards disappear fast.
