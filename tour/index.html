See the simulation in action

Five screens that show exactly what your class will experience. No sign-up, no email — just a 90-second look at the live multiplayer A-Level Business simulation.

01

Teacher Control Panel

Create a session. Get a 6-digit PIN. Set the pace.

Set up time.
Session starts. Three phases — you choose how long each runs. 5 min, 15 min, 30 min, or full 60.

The 6-digit PIN is generated instantly. Students go to education-tbs.co.uk, enter the PIN, and they're in. Three phases each, fully teacher-paced — you press Start when the class is ready, Skip Phase when you want to move on, End Game any time. There is no fixed length.

Flexible 15–60 min PIN-based access Teacher in control Projector view included
02

The Marketplace

Five firms competing. Real-time trading.

Every team launches their own products and trades with the others. "All firms trade with each other — that's the competitive core." Each decision affects both the team's own balance sheet and their competitors' market share. This is where students learn pricing, supply, and competitive strategy by doing — not by reading.

5 competing firms Real-time trading NPC + peer customers Pricing strategy live
03

Investments & Live Charts

Property + equities + sparklines.

Three property classes (residential, retail, commercial) with stable yields per round. Three equity stocks with live price ticks every 30–60 seconds and sparkline charts. Volatility profiles range from ±5% blue chip to ±10–15% high-risk tech. Real risk vs return — applied to A-Level finance theory the moment students see prices move.

Live price ticks Sparkline charts Volatility profiles Risk vs return
04

40 Business Scenarios

Real decisions, instant feedback.

A 40-question scenario library covering the situations every business actually faces — price objections, free-trial requests, budget pushback, unresponsive prospects, complaints, scope creep, payment terms. One attempt each. Correct answer earns +£500. Wrong answer costs −£200. Students think before they click — exactly how an A-Level evaluator wants them to think before writing.

40 ready-to-teach scenarios Instant feedback Real money stakes No prep needed
