What's inside

Question mix

4 short-answer (4–9 marks) · 4 application (10–12 marks) · 2 essay/evaluation (16–20 marks). Each question is mapped to a specific spec point on the cover.

Mark schemes

Indicative KAAE-structure mark schemes (Knowledge–Application–Analysis–Evaluation) modelled on AQA and Edexcel senior examiner reports.

Examiner tip on every question

Each question ends with the single most common reason students drop marks on that question type — written from the examiner's perspective, not the textbook's.

Bonus: 5 mark-loss patterns

The final page lists the five recurring patterns that cap students at lower levels — share with classes before the next mock.

Real company case studies

Every question uses a real UK or global business — no generic case studies, no AI fluff. Figures are simplified for classroom use and approximate where appropriate.

Greggs JD Sports Boohoo JCB Wetherspoon Shein (London IPO) Costa Coffee × Coca-Cola Tesla M&S cyber-attack Bristol vegan bakery (start-up case)

Spec coverage

AQA 7132: 3.1 What is business · 3.4 Decision making to improve operational, marketing, financial and HR performance · 3.5–3.8 Strategic position, direction, methods, managing strategic change.

Edexcel 9BS0: Theme 1 Marketing & people · Theme 2 Managing business activities · Theme 3 Business decisions & strategy · Theme 4 Global business.

How to use this pack

  • As mock paper material — pick 5 questions for a 90-minute mock; mark schemes ready.
  • As revision homework — assign one question per night, mark in class the next day using the indicative scheme.
  • As a final-week resource — print the bonus "5 patterns" page and walk students through it before the real exam.
  • Modify and remix — permitted for classroom use. Resell not permitted.
