Programme Overview

What this is — and what it isn't

The Pilot Programme exists to validate the simulation in real UK and international classrooms across the Autumn Term 2026, ahead of full release. Pilot schools receive the working classroom-ready build, run it with their students at their own pace, and provide a single end-of-term feedback survey. There is no cost, no licence fee, and no commitment to purchase afterwards.

What we deliver to your school

  • Working teacher licence for full 2026/27 academic year (no cost)
  • One-page IT info sheet for your network team (KCSIE 2024/25, UK GDPR)
  • Written getting-started guide (set-up steps, first-lesson suggestions, scheme-of-work fit)
  • Email support throughout the programme
  • Optional monthly drop-in office hour with the founder
  • Curriculum-mapping reference for AQA, Edexcel, OCR, BTEC, IGCSE and IB

What we ask of your school

  • Deploy the simulation with at least one Year 12 or Year 13 class between 15 September and 15 December 2026
  • Forward the IT info sheet to your network team for whitelisting
  • Complete a 5-minute end-of-term feedback survey in December 2026
  • Permission for us to reference your school by category (e.g. "UK independent sixth form") — never by name without separate consent
