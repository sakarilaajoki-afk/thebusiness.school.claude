Retention

Free tier sessions: ephemeral — auto-deleted within 24 hours of session end. Paid tier saved sessions: teacher-controlled deletion. Teacher accounts: retained during subscription, deleted within 30 days of termination. Server logs (IP): 30 days, auto-purged.

Sharing

No sale, no rental, no advertising profiling. The three sub-processors above process data solely to deliver their infrastructure or inference services. No marketing tracking cookies. PIN-code join eliminates credential risks.

Rights and complaints

Under UK GDPR / EU GDPR: rights of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and objection. Student data requests are routed via the school as Data Controller. Complaints to the UK ICO at ico.org.uk/concerns or to the Finnish Data Protection Ombudsman at tietosuoja.fi.
