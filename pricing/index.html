Questions

How is pricing different from per-student tools?
Many classroom tools charge £1–5 per student per year. At a full-intake Sixth Form that is £500–2,000 for Business Studies alone. A flat £249 Department licence is predictable and sits inside normal department capitation.
Why is there no "per-seat" tier?
Because it makes procurement harder. Most schools prefer a single-line invoice. The Department licence covers every student taught by that department in the licence year.
Does VAT apply?
TBS Education Ltd Oy is a Finnish company. UK-based schools are charged under the reverse-charge VAT mechanism (no VAT added at our invoice; the school accounts for it). We issue VAT-compliant invoices.
Can we try before we pay?
Yes. The pilot programme runs May–July 2026 free of charge. You pay from September if you continue.
How does the MAT discount work?
Five or more schools on a single agreement attract a per-school discount of 25–40% depending on size. One DPA covers the whole MAT. Contact us for a tailored quote.
What is the cancellation policy?
Cancel any time with 30 days' notice before renewal. No auto-upgrade; your tier stays the same unless you request a change.
