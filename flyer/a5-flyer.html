Why Heads and SLT bring us in

Three pressures UK Heads of Business face in 2026: the November 2025 Ofsted framework (post-16 now a standalone graded area), the AQA 7137/7138 spec refresh (first teaching Sep 2026), and tighter discretionary budgets. The Business School answers all three in a single 60-minute lesson.

What students do
  • Each student runs their own UK firm — buys stock, sets prices, manages a 20% VAT account
  • Six crisis events: Supply Crisis, Tax Hike, Recession, Viral Product, Demand Boom, New Competitor
  • Live auction bidding + aggressive-tactic dilemmas tied to Theme 3.4 evaluation
  • Auto-graded quiz, A* to U certificate, personalised AI performance report
What teachers get
  • No pupil accounts · PIN-based joining · one-click session start
  • Specification-tagged certificates and teacher CSV export for moderation
  • Projector view with live leaderboard, market pulse and ESG dilemma overlay
